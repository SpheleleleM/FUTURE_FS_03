Our Story

Born From the Braai

The Fire Restaurant was built on one simple belief — that great food starts with real fire. We're not a franchise. We're not a chain. We're a Johannesburg original, serving the kind of food that brings people together.

Every piece of meat that leaves our kitchen has been carefully selected, seasoned and braaied to perfection. Our pap is made fresh. Our sides are made with care. And our doors are always open — because hunger doesn't have a schedule.

Whether you're stopping in at noon or midnight, you'll always find a warm fire, a friendly face, and food that hits different.
