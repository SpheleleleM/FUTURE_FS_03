## Who Is The Business?

The Fire Restaurant is a 24-hour South African braai restaurant located at
9 Commissioner Street, Ferreiras Dorp, Johannesburg. They specialize in
authentic flame-grilled meat, pap, boerewors and traditional sides — the
kind of food that brings South Africans together.

What makes them stand out:
- Open 24 hours, 7 days a week, 365 days a year
- Authentic wood fire braai cooking — no shortcuts
- Best meat in the Johannesburg CBD area
- Centrally located and accessible to locals and commuters alike

---

## The Problem

The Fire Restaurant has no online presence. In 2026 this means:

- Potential customers search online and find nothing — so they go elsewhere
- There is no way for people to check the menu before visiting
- New customers don't know they are open 24 hours — their biggest advantage
- The restaurant cannot be found on Google searches for "braai near me"
- Word of mouth alone limits how far the business can grow
- Competitors with websites always appear more trustworthy and professional

A business with no website in today's world is essentially invisible online.

---

## The Solution

I designed and built a professional, mobile-friendly website for The Fire
Restaurant that solves every one of those problems.

**What the website includes:**

**Home Page**
A bold, fire-themed landing page that immediately communicates who they are,
where they are, and that they are open 24 hours. First impressions matter —
this one is unforgettable.

**Menu Section**
A clean, organized menu showing Mains, Sides and Drinks with descriptions
and prices. Customers can check the menu before visiting — which increases
the chance they will actually come in.

**About Section**
Tells the story of the restaurant, builds trust and makes the business feel
personal and authentic. People eat at places they trust.

**Contact & Location**
Phone number, full address, opening hours and an embedded Google Map — so
finding the restaurant takes zero effort for a new customer.

---

## How It Helps The Business

| Problem | How The Website Solves It |
|---|---|

---

## Business Benefits

**More customers**
People searching for "braai restaurant Johannesburg" or "food open 24 hours
Joburg" can now find The Fire Restaurant online.

**More trust**
A professional website makes a business look established and credible.
Customers are far more likely to visit a restaurant they found online with
a proper website than one they've never heard of.

**24/7 marketing**
The website works while the owners sleep. It answers common questions,
shows the menu and gives directions — without anyone needing to pick up
the phone.

**Competitive edge**
Most local braai spots in the area have no website or a poor one. This
website immediately positions The Fire Restaurant as the most professional
option.

**Shareable**
The owner can share one link on WhatsApp, Facebook or Instagram and
customers get everything they need — menu, location, contact and hours.
